Our Mission To You

The Cupcheck Cancer Tournament is a friendly indoor pond hockey style tournament held each year to raise money for Glioblastoma brain cancer research and awareness. It is also a time to honor the life of Kevin Wagner who loved the game of hockey. Our mission is to give back to those who like Kevin's family received so much help and love while battling brain cancer.
