Team Registration

BEFORE REGISTERING PLEASE VISIT THE TOURNAMENT PAGE AND READ ALL THE TOURNAMENT INFO AND RULES!!

To register, fill out the form below being sure to include the e-mail addresses of each team member as schedules and day-of-tournament info will be sent out via e-mail. A minimum of five players is needed including the team captain. If you have any questions, please contact us at cupcheckcancer@gmail.com

Payment MUST be made before a team is allowed to play. Scheduling priority will be given to those teams that pay in advance. You may choose to submit your registration and pay now via PayPal. If paying later, please use PayPal or Venmo. If you prefer to pay by check, make the check out to Cole Simmons with "Cupcheck Cancer Tournament" in the memo and bring it to the tournament.
